And the "Hamilton" actor for Lafayette did not throw away his shot.

Here's an example of one of his most difficult verses:

And I’m never gonna stop until I make ‘em / Drop and burn ‘em up and scatter their remains

What makes it difficult is that it's the fastest line ever spoken on Broadway, according to analysis form FiveThirtyEight.

And just how fast?

6.3 words per second

Belmont student Chris Lee stepped into the big shoes of Daveed Diggs, a professional rapper, to the thunderous applause of the Chicago audience when he rapped that record-breaking line. Major props to Chicago's Lafayette character!
